An awesome space shooting game

Planetoids is a little game I made in my first year of DAE. It's a lot like astroids yet with unique movement controls and lasers. When your little spacecraft shoots a laser it slices the astroids in half.

I later remade this little game using the html5 canvas, so everyone can play it in their browsers without any plugin. That's what you're seeing above. It uses Box2dweb for physics.
